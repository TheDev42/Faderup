Services

Technical Drawings

We produce detailed and precise technical drawings that serve as blueprints for the implementation of our lighting designs. 

Concept Creation

We craft unique and innovative lighting concepts that enhance the visual appeal and atmosphere of any space or event. 

Visual Renders

We create realistic 3D and video renders that visually represent our lighting concepts, allowing clients to preview and refine designs before implementation. 

Timecoded Shows

We program sophisticated, timecoded light shows that synchronize with music or other event elements.

Live Lighting

Our team ensures that the lighting enhances the live experience, responding in real-time to the energy and flow of the event.
